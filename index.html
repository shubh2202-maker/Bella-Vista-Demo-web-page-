Our Story

Bella Vista, meaning Beautiful View,
is a family-owned restaurant in Fruit Cove, Florida.

Owner Giovanni started working in restaurants
and upscale hotels as a teenager,
near his hometown of Sicignano degli Alburni,
in South Italy.

There, he developed a deep passion
for the food service industry
and dreamed of owning his own restaurant one day.

After graduating from a 5-year culinary program,
Giovanni moved to New York City.

He worked at various restaurants
and as a personal chef,
bringing his customers a true taste
of Italian hospitality.
